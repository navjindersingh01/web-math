Arithmetic Series (AP)

Formula: Sₙ = n/2 (2a + (n-1)d)

Geometric Series (GP)

Formula: Sₙ = a(1 - rⁿ)/(1 - r)

Infinite Geometric Series

Formula: S = a / (1 - r)

Condition: |r| < 1
